LEMON CATCH v1.1 — DEPLOY TO VERCEL
=====================================

STEP 1: Go to vercel.com → log in (or create free account with Google)

STEP 2: Click "Add New Project"

STEP 3: Click "Upload" and drag the entire "lemon-catch" folder in

STEP 4: Vercel auto-detects everything. Just click "Deploy"

STEP 5: Wait ~1 minute. You'll get a link like: lemon-catch.vercel.app

STEP 6: On your phone, open the link in Safari
        → Tap Share button → "Add to Home Screen" → "Add"

Done! It lives on your home screen like a real app. 🍋

TO SHARE WITH TESTERS:
Just send them the Vercel link. Each person's data stays on their device.
